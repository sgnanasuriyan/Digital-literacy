# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

| Do's | Don'ts |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Do** use privacy settings to control who can see your personal posts. | **Don't** post confidential information about school or work. |
| **Do** think before you post; assume everything online is permanent. | **Don't** engage with "trolls" or participate in harassment. |
| **Do** keep your professional bios up to date. | **Don't** post content involving illegal activities or excessive partying. |
| **Do** network with professionals and alumni in your field. | **Don't** vent about colleagues or employers publicly. |
| **Do** verify facts before sharing news. | **Don't** use offensive language or promote discrimination. |
